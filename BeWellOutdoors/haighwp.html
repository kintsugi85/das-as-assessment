Haigh High Ropes and Adventure

Taking adventure to another level

Soar among the treetops at Haigh Woodland Park on our thrilling high ropes course or get closer to nature with enriching woodland activities designed to encourage a spirit of adventure.

High Ropes Course

With 11 obstacles to complete, our high ropes course is designed challenge and excite even the most adventurous thrill-seeker ahead of facing the exhilarating finale of our 40-metre zip wire! You can book sessions and check availability through Eventbrite.
