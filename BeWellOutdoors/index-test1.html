Providing fun and educational outdoor adventures in the north west

Be Well Outdoors has been a trusted provider of outdoor education trips and residentials for many years - helping generations of young people and adventure seekers to discover and explore a passion for the great outdoors.

Owned by Wigan Council, we operate to the highest levels of safety and standards and are committed to the exciting and ambitious Deal 2030 strategy which aims to deliver the very best health, wellbeing, and environmental outcomes for all.
