School Trips

Where learning becomes a real-life adventure

From residential stays and day trips to on-site visits, we provide outdoor education which builds confidence and independence in natural environments for primary and secondary school pupils.

Working together, stepping outside your comfort zone, experiencing things you’ve never experienced before and achieving what you thought was impossible – this is what Be Well Outdoors is all about.

Residentials in the Lake District

Our school residentials at Low Bank Ground provide high quality outdoor learning experiences in an exciting and stimulating environment for or KS1 and KS2 students.

Led by experienced and qualified outdoor instructors, they are designed to challenge and thrill, giving young people confidence while teaching essential life skills.

We work with schools, colleges and youth groups to create bespoke packages that align with activity preferences and budgets, creating the best possible experience for your class and meeting specific curriculum learning objectives.

Our school residentials stays are fully catered – our on-site chef will prepare healthy, wholesome meals including breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks.

School Visits – Bringing the outdoors to you!

We believe every young person deserves the chance to experience the world of outdoor adventure – so when you’re not able to come to us, we’ll bring a taste of the outdoors to you!

Learn about outdoor survival with essential bushcraft skills and enjoy natural eco-crafts and other woodland activities, or scale new heights on our climbing tower. Whatever your budget we’ll work with you to create one-off and longer-term in-school programmes delivered by our expert instructors.
