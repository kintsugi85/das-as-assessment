Scotman's Flash

NEED TITLE

This hidden gem a short distance from Wigan town centre is the perfect setting to dip your toe into the world of watersports. Available for hire sessions, school trips, corporate bookings and parties.

Dive into the thrills and spills of watersports

Discover our Watersports Activity Centre at the heart of a genuine national treasure, the beautiful and unique Flashes of Wigan and Leigh National Nature Reserve. Here we offer a range of watersports, coaching and courses for people of all abilities - delivered by fully qualified and friendly instructors who are on hand to help you, guide you, and ensure your safety.

Whether you're on your own or part of a group, a complete novice or already wet behind the ears, there's so much to enjoy… from stand up paddleboarding and kayaking to sailing and windsurfing. Hire a rafted canoe or up your skills with our Level 2 powerboat courses. Can't make up your mind? We can even tailor an itinerary of combined activities just for you!

Booking is essential at least 24 hours in advance.

What you'll need

Remember to bring swimwear, a towel, spare footwear (no flips flops though!) and a change of clothes.

If you own a wetsuit, you’re free to bring that too – we only hire out wetsuits when the water temperature drops in autumn.

Please avoid wearing heavy clothes such as a hooded jumper. If they get wet they will make you cold and weigh you down in the water!

We provide buoyancy aids and lifejackets on your arrival as part of the hire price. We also provide security boxes for any valuables.

Our Scotman's Flash outdoor education team is approved as a provider by the Adventure Activities Licensing Authority (AALA) and RYA training centre.
