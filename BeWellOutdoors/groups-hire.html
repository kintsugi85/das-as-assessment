Groups & Hire

We provide the adventure, you make the memories

Whether you’re looking for a day out with a difference, planning a fun family getaway or a special celebration, you can do it with Be Well Outdoors.

Scotman's Flash

Go paddleboarding, kayaking, canoeing or sailing on Wigan's hidden gem. Hire, lessons and courses are available or you can bring your own*.

You can book sessions and check availability through Eventbrite.

Book a session Find out more about Scotman's Flash

Haigh High Ropes & Adventure

Rope in friends and family for some high-octane fun at Haigh Woodland Park on our low and high rope obstacle courses or get a taste of the outdoors with bushcraft, den building and other woodland activities.

You can book sessions and check availability through Eventbrite.

Book a session Find out more about Haigh Woodland Park

Low Bank Ground

The perfect place for group getaways with a difference, welcoming groups of 12-50. Enjoy hill walking, ghyll scrambling, rock climbing, water sports and a whole lot more in the heart of the Lake District. Whatever you’re interested in, we’ll build a bespoke package for you.

Make an enquiry Find out more about Low Bank Ground

Action-packed birthday parties

If you’re looking to plan a party with a difference, you've come to the right place. Make a splash at Scotman’s Flash, take fun to new heights on our rope courses at Haigh Woodland Park or plan that special celebration in the lakes at Low Bank Ground.

Our expert team is on hand to design a bespoke action-packed birthday bash creating memories you’ll never forget.
